Internet Privacy Policy

This website is owned and operated by L A Polymer Consulting. We are committed to high ethical standards in our business practices and respect our clients privacy and confidentiality of information. Information is collected two ways, information that you volunteer and information which is collected automatically. Unless you volunteer to submit personal data yourself, L A Polymer Consulting will not collect any information from which individual visitors can be identified. This privacy statement outlines what information is collected and how it may be used.

Information Volunteered

We will not share, disclose or sell any non-public information to any persons or organizations without your written consent with the exception of courts of law or law enforcement agencies. Additionally, we will not share any information which might cause a conflict of interest, be deemed collusion, or other for any unethical purposes.

If you elect to provide personal information (e.g. name, address, telephone number, and e-mail address), L A Polymer Consulting will not disclose, sell, rent or exchange such personally identifiable information to any other organization or entity unless the user is first notified and expressly agrees to it, or as otherwise required by law.

We will never knowingly request personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13 without requesting parental consent consistent with the Federal Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act of 1988 (COPPA).

We appreciate client feedback and reserve the right to use such comments in marketing materials, advertising and promotions. When using any feedback, we will not disclose any non-public information.

Automatically Collected Information

L A Polymer Consulting may track user traffic pattern throughout our site to measure the use of and to optimize the site to better service our client base. We do not correlate this information with data about individual users.

Our website does not utilize tracking cookies or download any programs to your computer.

Security and Consent

This site may contain links to other web sites. L A Polymer Consulting shall not be responsible for the content and privacy practices of these other website.

L A Polymer Consulting reserves its right to amend this policy at any time. The privacy policy does not create any contractual or other legal right in or on behalf of any party.
